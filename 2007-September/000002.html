Hello Tumen,

probably it is better for you to use either "General-->Resize Image",
or "General-->Presumming" which average your SLC's if you provide
smaller image sizes for your data.
Take care that multi--looking on the matrices and the vectors are two
very different operations: they are not interchangeable and do have
very different meanings.
The function for transforming from a matrix to vector was intended
only for rank 1 matrices (i.e. no multi--looking before), and
therefore it shouldn't be used with MLC matrices (we should have noted
it somewhere in the docu!), and it is neither really reasonable.
