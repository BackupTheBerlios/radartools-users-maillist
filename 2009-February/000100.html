Gents,
I had the same problem with the icons. As Andreas already suggested I merely
copied the "Icons" directory, from rat_v0.20 to the User\username path in the
root (C:\) this solved the strange toolbar.

I've had no problems opening ASAR APP data nor WSM but have not tried SLC data.
For the record Radarsat-2 and ALOS PALSAR work without problems (quad-pol).
In fact my Vista installation works fine and I notice no appreciable difference
between it nd my Win XP installation.

Great software by the way; thanks!
