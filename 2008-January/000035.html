yes, it seems like we need to look closer at the coregistration procedures
in the near future.

The behavior of the coarse registration procedure is as intended. It just
shifts/rotates the slave image with the given offset, where the wraped parts
are added at the other side. However, I just modified this and in the new
version one will have the choice to just shift, or to set the offset pixels
to zero, or to cut them out.

Thank you for your comments,
Maxim
