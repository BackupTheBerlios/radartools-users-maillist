Maxim is right, RAT in general is not really prepared to handle dual-pol data. 
However, you can try to double one of the two channels in order to simulate a 
full-pol dataset. Maybe it works, but I won't expect much. 

Anyway, ASAR-APP data is _not_ really polarimetric, basically you get only 2 
valid amplitude channels, the interchannel phase is pure noise. Therefore, a 
Wishart classification does not make much sense...
