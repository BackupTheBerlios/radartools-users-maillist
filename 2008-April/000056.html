Hi Londo,

You can open a tiff in RAT with File->Open pixmap->Open TIFF

However, this is certainly not optimal. And, geographic information will be 
lost because RAT is currently not handling these kind of data.

Alternatively, there is ENVI (expensive...), which can, as far as I know, 
calculate the Haralik GLCM parameters.
