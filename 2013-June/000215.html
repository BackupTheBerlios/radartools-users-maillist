Dear All,
As far as I can tell, and as I am sure many of you have noticed, RAT doesn't work on 64-bit (Win) Operating Systems. I've tried several machines using data I know has worked in the past, without any luck. 32-bit machines are getting hard to find so soon I'll lose the ability to process data in RAT. So, here are two questions for you:

1.       Does anyone have a work around for RAT on &4-bit Win machines?

2.       Do Andreas, Max or any of the original programmers plan to port RAT over to 64-bit OSs or is anyone else planning to?

It's a great program, particularly for PolSAR, and it'd be a shame to lose it.
