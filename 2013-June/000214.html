Dear Mariane,
The range resolution is typically worse than azimuth by a factor of as much as 8 for ALOS PALSAR. This means to create (nearly) square pixels you will have to resample. This is often done during multi-looking and would mean a reduction of the azimuth resolution by a factor of 7 or 8. If you reduce the range resolution to the azimuth resolution you are in effect oversampling in range. You might get square pixels but they will be artificially high resolution.

I hope that answers your question.
Ian
