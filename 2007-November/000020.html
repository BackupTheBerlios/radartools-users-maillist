Hi,

I don't know if you got a direct reply from the guy who programmed this 
module, but in short he told me that very likely your data are not correctly 
calibrated. If your HV channel has not been divided by sqrt(2) during 
calibration, almost everything in your scene will be treated as volume. Do 
you get only one green colour or several of them?
