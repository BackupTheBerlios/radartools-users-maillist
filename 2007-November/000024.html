Hi Sven,

This time the data are mirrored in azimuth, but not in range. EV-APP seems to 
do always try to have the data approximately with north up and east-west not 
mirrored. However, this is bad for idiot, because again the alignment between 
data and DEM goes wrong. One result is the strange looking slave image, but 
of course this is only one of many bad effects.

An azimuth mirror is more complicated to correct because I have to read the 
entire data backwards, not only the individual lines. Therefore I'll probably 
need some time until I find a calm moment to work on this. It's not really 
hard to implement, but more than 3 lines to change - just be a bit patient...

Greetings,
Andreas
