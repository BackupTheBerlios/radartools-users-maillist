Hi Tumen,

Did you speckle filter your covariances before performing the Moriyama 
decomposition? If not, i.e. if the covariances are singular, strange results 
might occur.

Please use "General->Presumming" with at least 4x4, or "PolSAR->Speckle 
Filtering" with a 5x5 or 7x7 box. If then the problem is still present, let 
us know...

Good luck,
Andreas
