Hi Waleed,

In simple terms, to obtain height information from the interferometric phase
you need to scale it (after range filtering, etc.) with the reciprocal of
the vertical wavenumber (kz):
relative height = phase / kz
kz is given by the acquisition system geometry:
kz = 2 k0 Bp / (r0 sin(inc))
where k0 is the wavenumber, Bp is the perpendicular baseline, r0 the range
distance, inc the incidence angle.
Concerning the errors with rat: probably you are not in the main rat
directory, and because of this idl cannot find the functions. Hence, change
to this directory, e.g. with the idl command "cd":
idl>   cd, "MainRatDir"

Regards,
Maxim
