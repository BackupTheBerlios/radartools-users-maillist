Dear All,
this discussion leads me to wonder if we could either: 
-establish a 'knowledge base' for RAT functions and processing chains (to
supplement the manual and thereby help the developers who have put in so muh
time and effort to develop this excellent resource),
and/or,
-provide details on complementary processing with third party software. As Drs
Rao and Perski note ESA provides very good software that I have found
complements RAT very well. I still find though that RAT is the best solution for
many specific operations. Were we to create such a knowledge base I would also
include details on import/export and comparisons with mainstream software such
as PCIs SPW.

You may think this is unnecessary or uninteresting and I would welcome your comments
